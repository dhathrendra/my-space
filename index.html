About Me

I’m Dhathrendra, but you can call me Dhath. Currently pursuing my Bachelor’s at IIT Madras, diving deep into the intersection of neuroscience, psychology, biology, and programming. I’m obsessed with how the human brain works - and how we can mirror biological intelligence in machines.

On campus, I’m heavily involved with Shaastra, CFI clubs, and the Debating Society. These roles have sharpened my skills in leadership, event management, networking, and building high-energy teams that actually get work done.

Outside the classroom, I’m a perpetual learner - always chasing new challenges. You’ll find me reading, hitting the gym, traveling, or just chilling with family and friends. Born and raised in Hyderabad - yes, I treat biryani like religion and can disappear for hours into music, movies, and anime.

Long-term? I want to build things that sit at the crossroads of biology, tech, and real-world impact. If it’s meaningful and pushes boundaries - I’m in. Let’s connect and create something legendary.
